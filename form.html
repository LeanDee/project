please read rules and regulations before booking an appointment.

Advance Booking: Clients must book their appointments in advance. Walk-in appointments may not be available or may be subject to availability. Cancellation Policy: Clients should adhere to the studio's cancellation policy, which may include notifying the studio within a certain timeframe (e.g., 24 hours) to cancel or reschedule an appointment. Failure to do so may result in a cancellation fee or loss of the appointment slot. Arrival Time: Clients should arrive at the studio at least 10-15 minutes before their scheduled appointment to check-in, settle any payments, and prepare for the session. Hygiene and Attire: Clients should wear appropriate attire for yoga practice and maintain personal hygiene, including clean clothes and minimal use of strong scents or perfumes. Health Declaration: Clients may be required to fill out a health declaration form before attending their appointment, disclosing any medical conditions, injuries, or other relevant information. Respect for Others: Clients should respect the studio environment and other practitioners by maintaining a quiet and focused atmosphere, refraining from disruptive behavior or conversations during classes or appointments. Payment: Payment for appointments should be made in advance or upon arrival at the studio, as per the studio's payment policy. Accepted payment methods may include cash, credit/debit cards, or digital payments. Limitations and Restrictions: Clients should inform the studio of any physical limitations, injuries, or medical conditions that may affect their ability to participate in certain yoga practices or poses. Equipment Use: Clients should handle studio equipment with care and return it to its designated place after use. Mats, blocks, straps, and other props may be provided by the studio for use during appointments. Studio Policies: Clients are expected to familiarize themselves with and abide by the studio's terms and conditions, rules, and regulations, which may be communicated through the studio's website, booking platform, or posted within the facility. Feedback and Communication: Clients are encouraged to provide feedback to the studio regarding their experience, including any concerns, suggestions, or compliments, to help improve the quality of service.
